What is a food diary?

A food diary is a way to keep track of what you eat, and ensure that you're getting enough of the right nutrients. It's a way to track your caloric intake and can be useful for both losing and gaining weight.

Features

Secure Diary: Your diary is your business, nobody but you can access it.

Persistent Data: Your data is there whenever you need it. All records are saved in a secure database powered by SQL.

Easy to use: Viewing, adding, and editing your entries is a breeze

The Tools You Need: Apart from the diary we also boast a number of fitness calculators to help you along.
